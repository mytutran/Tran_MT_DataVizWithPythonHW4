WOMEN OF CANADA ARE GROWING STRONG IN ICE HOCKEY

WOMEN OF CANADA
ARE GROWING STRONG IN
ICE HOCKEY

Women of Canada did not start participate in Olympics Winter in Ice Hockey, until 1998. The data visualizations in this design are to show how well Canadian women have been doing to catch up with men and to compare the medals counts between women in Canada and around the world.

Medal counts of women and men in Canada throughout the time and the comparison of total achieved medals

Men had a stable count of medals: 3 terms continuously achieved 21 medals in total. Women, however still have not achieved more medals than men, but they showed a rapid increase thoughout the period, in which they achieved only 4 medals less than men in 2014...

The proportion of every type of medal achieved by each gender

With the provided values, it was shown that men only achieved gold medals since 1998, and women only achieved gold and silver medals, there was no bronze medals on both sides...

Comparing to other countries

Comparing to other women from different countries, women in Canada had achieved a significant amount of medals, which it was only 0.3% lesser than USA. However, women in Canada had the most gold medals in Ice Hockey...
